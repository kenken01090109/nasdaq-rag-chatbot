Business Summary: Forte Biosciences, Inc. operates as a clinical-stage biopharmaceutical company in the United States. The company's lead product candidate is the FB-102, a proprietary anti-CD122 monoclonal antibody therapeutic candidate for various autoimmune and autoimmune-related indications, such as graft-vs-host disease, vitiligo, and alopecia areata, as well as for celiac disease and non-segmental vitiligo. Forte Biosciences, Inc. is headquartered in Dallas, Texas.
