Business Summary: SSR Mining Inc., together with its subsidiaries, engages in the acquisition, exploration, and development of precious metal resource properties in the United States, Türkiye, Canada, and Argentina. The company explores for gold doré, copper, silver, lead, and zinc deposits. It holds interests in the Çöpler located in Erzincan province, Turkey; the Marigold situated in Nevada, the United States; the Seabee located in Saskatchewan, Canada; and the Puna situated in Jujuy province, Argentina. The company was formerly known as Silver Standard Resources Inc. and changed its name to SSR Mining Inc. in August 2017. SSR Mining Inc. was incorporated in 1946 and is based in Denver, Colorado.
