Business Summary: Lead Real Estate Co., Ltd together with its subsidiaries, develops and sells luxury residential properties in Japan. The company develops single-family homes and condominiums. It also operates hotels in Tokyo and leases apartment building units to individuals in Japan and Dallas, Texas. In addition, the company engages in master lease business which includes fixed term building lease agreements between owners of extended-stay type hotels and the Company. Lead Real Estate Co., Ltd was founded in 2001 and is headquartered in Tokyo, Japan.
