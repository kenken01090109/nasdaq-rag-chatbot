Business Summary: enGene Holdings Inc., through its subsidiary enGene, Inc., operates as a clinical-stage biotechnology company in Canada. The company develops genetic medicines to help patients suffering from bladder cancer. Its lead product candidate is detalimogene voraplasmid, a monotherapy for the treatment of non-muscle invasive bladder cancer (NMIBC). Its pipeline includes LEGEND, which is in Phase 2. enGene Holdings Inc. is based in Montreal, Canada.
