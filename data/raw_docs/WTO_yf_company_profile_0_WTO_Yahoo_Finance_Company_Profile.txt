Business Summary: UTime Limited, together with its subsidiaries, designs, develops, manufactures, sells, and operates mobile phones, accessories, and related consumer electronics. The company offers consumer electronics, such as power banks and Bluetooth speakers, as well as spare parts, such as batteries, chargers, cell phone parts, molds, and shells. It also provides electronics manufacturing services, including original equipment manufacturer and original design manufacturer services. It sells its products under the UTime and Do brand names in People's Republic of China, Hong Kong, Japan, Africa, the United States, and internationally. UTime Limited was founded in 2008 and is headquartered in Shenzhen, China.
