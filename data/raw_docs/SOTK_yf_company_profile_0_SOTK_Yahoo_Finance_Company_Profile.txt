Business Summary: Sono-Tek Corporation designs and manufactures ultrasonic coating systems for applying on parts and components for the microelectronics/electronics, alternative energy, medical, industrial, and research and development/other markets. The company offers custom-engineered ultrasonic coating systems; and nozzles and generators. It also provides integrated multi-axis coating systems, integrated coating systems, fluxing systems, OEM systems, and other related systems. In addition, the company offers surface coating solutions and application consulting services. It markets and distributes its products through direct sales personnel, select independent distributors, and sales representatives. The company operates in the United States, Canada, the Asia Pacific, Europe, the Middle East, Asia, and Latin America. Sono-Tek Corporation was incorporated in 1975 and is headquartered in Milton, New York.
