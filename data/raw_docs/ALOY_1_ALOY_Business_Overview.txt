Company: REalloys Inc. Common Stock
Ticker: ALOY
Business Summary: REalloys Inc. operates as a rare earth metals and permanent magnet company in North America. The company produces rare earth metals, such as neodymium, praseodymium, dysprosium, terbium, samarium, gadolinium, yttrium, and scandium; and magnets, such as NdFeB Magnets, SmFe12 Magnets, and MnBi Magnets. The company was founded in 2024 and is headquartered in Euclid, Ohio.
Sector: Basic Materials
Industry: Other Industrial Metals & Mining
