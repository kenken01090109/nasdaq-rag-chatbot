Business Summary: Reservoir Media, Inc. operates as a music publishing company. The company operates through two segments, Music Publishing and Recorded Music. The Music Publishing segment acquires interests in music catalogs, as well as signs songwriters. The Recorded Music segment engages in the acquisition of sound recording catalogs; discovery and development of recording artists; and marketing, distribution, sale, and licensing of the music catalogs. Reservoir Media, Inc. was founded in 2007 and is headquartered in New York, New York.
