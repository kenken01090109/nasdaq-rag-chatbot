Business Summary: Vroom, Inc., through its subsidiaries, operates as an automotive finance company. The company offers vehicle financing to its customers through third party dealers under the UACC brand. It also provides an artificial intelligence-powered analytics and digital services platform for automotive retail. The company was formerly known as Auto America, Inc. and changed its name to Vroom, Inc. in July 2015. Vroom, Inc. was incorporated in 2012 and is based in Fort Worth, Texas.
