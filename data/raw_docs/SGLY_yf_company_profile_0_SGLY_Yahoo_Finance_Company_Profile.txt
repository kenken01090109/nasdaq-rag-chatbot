Business Summary: Singularity Future Technology Ltd. operates as an integrated logistics solutions provider in China and the United States. It offers transportation, warehouse, collection, last-mile delivery, drop shipping, customs clearance, and overseas transit delivery services. The company also offers freight logistics services, including shipping, warehouse and other logistical support services. In addition, it serves the steel companies businesses. The company was formerly known as Sino-Global Shipping America, Ltd. and changed its name to Singularity Future Technology Ltd. in January 2022. Singularity Future Technology Ltd. was founded in 2001 and is headquartered in New York, New York.
