Business Summary: Integrated Media Technology Limited, a technology investment, product development, and distribution company, engages in the manufacture and sale of nano coated plates for filters and air purifiers. The company is involved in the manufacture and sale of electronic glass; provision of halal certification; distribution of Halal products and new energy products and services; and operation of Ouction, a marketplace for trading in digital assets. It also sells smart glass products and smart devices, as well as air filer products. The company was formerly known as China Integrated Media Corporation Limited and changed its name to Integrated Media Technology Limited in October 2016. Integrated Media Technology Limited was incorporated in 2008 and is headquartered in Adelaide, Australia.
