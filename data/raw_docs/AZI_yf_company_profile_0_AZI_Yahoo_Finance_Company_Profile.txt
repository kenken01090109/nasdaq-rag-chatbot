Business Summary: Autozi Internet Technology (Global) Ltd., together with its subsidiaries, provides automotive products and services through online and offline channels in the People's Republic of China. The company sells new cars, and auto parts and accessories. It offers automotive insurance-related services, including claim and repair, and insurance intermediation services, as well as value-added maintenance services through its Autozi Car Owner platform. It serves MBS stores, passenger car buyers and owners, and insurance companies. The company was founded in 2010 and is headquartered in Beijing, China.
