Business Summary: Empery Digital Inc. designs, develops, and sells electric off road powersport vehicles in the United States. The company provides electric two-wheeled e-bikes and motorcycles; four-wheeled utility terrain vehicles; and golf carts, as well as various upgrades and accessories. It sells its products through powersports dealers, bicycle retailers, and golf cart dealers, as well as through its website. Empery Digital Inc. was formerly known as Volcon, Inc. and changed its name to Empery Digital Inc. in July 2025. Empery Digital Inc. was incorporated in 2020 and is based in Round Rock, Texas.
