Business Summary: Forafric Global PLC engages in the purchase, storage, transport, processing, and sale of agricultural commodities and commodity products in Morocco, Burkina, Mali, and internationally. The company operates through Soft Wheat, Durum Wheat, and Couscous and Pasta segments. It provides wheat flours and semolina; and secondary processing products, such as pasta and couscous. The company operates under the TRIA and MayMouna brand names. It serves wholesale foods manufacturers and distributors. The company also exports its products to approximately 45 countries. Forafric Global PLC was founded in 1926 and is headquartered in Gibraltar.
