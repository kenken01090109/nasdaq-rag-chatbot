Business Summary: Savara Inc., a clinical stage biopharmaceutical company, focuses on rare respiratory diseases. The company's lead product candidate is molgramostim, an inhaled granulocyte-macrophage colony-stimulating factor, which is in Phase 3 development stage for the treatment of autoimmune pulmonary alveolar proteinosis. The company was founded in 2007 and is headquartered in Langhorne, Pennsylvania.
