Business Summary: Precipio, Inc., a healthcare biotechnology company, provides cancer diagnostic products, reagents, and services in the United States. It provides diagnostic blood cancer testing services. The company offers IV-Cell, a proprietary cell culture media that enables simultaneous culturing of four hematopoietic cell lineages; and HemeScreen, a suite of robust genetic diagnostic panels. It offers biomarker testing and clinical project services to bio-pharma customers. Precipio, Inc. is based in New Haven, Connecticut.
