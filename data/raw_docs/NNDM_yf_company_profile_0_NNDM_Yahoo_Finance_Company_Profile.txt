Business Summary: Nano Dimension Ltd., together with its subsidiaries, provides industrial manufacturing solutions for design-to-manufacturing of electronics and mechanical parts in the Americas, the Asia Pacific, Europe, the Middle East, and Africa. Its product groups include advanced hydraulic systems; resins; dental parts services; binder jetting products for metal and sandcasting; additively manufactured electronics; additive manufacturing digital light processing-based systems for industrial and health applications; surface-mount technology products; digital printing sub-components hardware and software; and desktop and industrial fused filament fabrication solutions. The company sells its products to aerospace, defense, automotive, electronics, medical, research, and academia, as well as government organizations. Nano Dimension Ltd. is headquartered in Waltham, Massachusetts.
