Business Summary: Coincheck Group N.V. provides cryptocurrency exchange platform in Japan. It operates Coincheck NFT Marketplace that enables non-fungible tokens to be purchased. Coincheck Group N.V. operates as a subsidiary of Monex Group, Inc.
