Business Summary: Citius Pharmaceuticals, Inc., a biopharmaceutical company, focuses on the development and commercialization of critical care products. Its LYMPHIR, an FDA-approved product, is a targeted immunotherapy for the treatment of cutaneous T-cell lymphoma. The company's late-stage pipeline includes Mino-Lok, an antibiotic lock solution to treat patients with catheter-related bloodstream infections; Halo-Lido, a corticosteroid-lidocaine topical formulation to provide anti-inflammatory and anesthetic relief to persons suffering from hemorrhoids; and NoveCite, a mesenchymal stem cell therapy for the treatment of ARDS. Citius Pharmaceuticals, Inc. was founded in 2007 and is headquartered in Cranford, New Jersey.
