Business Summary: Grande Group Limited, through its subsidiaries, provides corporate finance advisory services in Hong Kong, the People's Republic of China, and Singapore. It offers IPO sponsorship, takeovers code, and other regulatory compliance related services; referral, underwriting and placing services; and general advisory, independent financial, and compliance advisory services. The company was formerly known as Hero Intelligence Group Limited and changed its name to Grande Group Limited in May 2024. The company was incorporated in 2020 and is based in Admiralty, Hong Kong. Grande Group Limited operates as a subsidiary of Grande Holding Limited.
