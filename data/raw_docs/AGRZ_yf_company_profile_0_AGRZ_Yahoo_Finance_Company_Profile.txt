Business Summary: Agroz Inc., an investment holding company, operates as a vertically integrated agricultural technology company in Malaysia. The company focuses on designing, developing, building, operating, and managing large, commercial scale, and industrial grade indoor CEA vertical farms, including green butterhead, red butterhead, green coral, red coral, green oak, wild rocket, green kale, arugula, and others. The company was founded in 2020 and is based in Petaling Jaya, Malaysia.
