Business Summary: Fold Holdings, Inc., a bitcoin financial services company, provides access to bitcoin through a suite of consumer financial products in the United States. Its financial services platform allows consumers to accumulate, save, and use bitcoin to accomplish financial goals. It offers consumers an FDIC insured checking account, a Visa prepaid debit card, bill payments, and an extensive catalog of merchant reward offers, as well as partners with third-party service providers that offer bitcoin exchange and custody services. The company also invests in and accumulates bitcoin for treasury. The company offers its products and services through the Fold mobile app. Fold Holdings, Inc. was founded in 2019 and is headquartered in Phoenix, Arizona.
