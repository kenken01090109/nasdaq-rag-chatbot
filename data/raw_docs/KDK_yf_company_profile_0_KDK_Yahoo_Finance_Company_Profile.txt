Business Summary: Kodiak AI, Inc. engages in the development of autonomous vehicle technology and related services. The company offers Kodiak Driver, a unified platform driving autonomy forward across trucking. It also provides oversight and integration tools, including Kodiak OnTime, a proprietary suite of support services and tools designed to provide smooth integration with customer operations. In addition, the company delivers freight through its autonomous trucks; and offers driver-as-a-service to its customers. It serves commercial trucking and public sectors. Kodiak AI, Inc. was founded in 2018 and is headquartered in Mountain View, California.
