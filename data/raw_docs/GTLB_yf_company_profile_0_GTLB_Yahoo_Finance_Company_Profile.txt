Business Summary: GitLab Inc., together with its subsidiaries, develops software for the software development lifecycle in the United States, Europe, and the Asia Pacific. The company provides GitLab that covers various stages of the DevSecOps lifecycle, including project planning, source code management, integration, application security testing, packaging artifacts, delivery and deployment, infrastructure for optimal deployment, monitoring for incidents, protecting the production deployment, and managing the cycle with value stream analytics. Its platform allows customers to manage and secure their applications across various cloud through a single platform. The company also helps organizations to plan, develop, secure, and deploy software. In addition, it offers related training and professional services. The company was formerly known as GitLab B.V. and changed its name to GitLab Inc. in July 2015. GitLab Inc. was founded in 2011 and is headquartered in San Francisco, California.
