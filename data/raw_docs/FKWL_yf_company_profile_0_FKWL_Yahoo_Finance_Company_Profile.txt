Business Summary: Franklin Wireless Corp. provides integrated wireless solutions in North America and Asia. The company offers 5G/4G wireless broadband products, such as portable wi-fi mobile hotspot routers, mobile hotspots, fixed wireless routers, and mobile device management (MDM) solutions. It also provides mobile device management that ensure security, compliance, and efficient operation across mobile environments; and network management systems, for monitoring, managing, and optimizing network performance. In addition, the company offers quvo family guardian solutions, including parental control and senior care. The company markets and sells its products to wireless operators, strategic partners, and distributors. Franklin Wireless Corp. was founded in 1981 and is headquartered in San Diego, California.
