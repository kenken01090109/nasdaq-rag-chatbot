Business Summary: Expensify, Inc. provides a cloud-based expense management software platform in the United States and internationally. The company offers Expensify, a platform that engages in managing corporate cards, paying bills, generating invoices, collecting payments, and booking travel, as well as track and submit plans for individuals. It serves individuals and corporations, small and midsized businesses, and enterprises. The company was founded in 2008 and is based in San Francisco, California.
