Business Summary: Profusa, Inc. develops bioengineered sensors for body to detect and continuously transmit clinical-grade data for personal and medical use in the United States. It offers the Lumee Oxygen Platform to monitor compromised tissue for peripheral artery disease, chronic wounds, and reconstructive surgery. The company researches and develops the Lumee Glucose Platform which is designed to monitor glucose levels in interstitial fluid. Profusa, Inc. is based in Berkeley, California.
