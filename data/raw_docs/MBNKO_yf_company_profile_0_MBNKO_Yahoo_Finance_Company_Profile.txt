Business Summary: Medallion Bank, an industrial bank, originates consumer loans, raises deposits, and conducts other banking activities to individuals and businesses in the United States. It offers consumer loans through dealers and financial service providers (FSPs) for the purchase of recreation products, such as recreational vehicles and boats; loans through contractors and FSPs for the purchase of home improvements, such as replacement windows and roofs; and loan origination services to fintech partners. The company was incorporated in 2002 and is based in Salt Lake City, Utah. Medallion Bank operates as a subsidiary of Medallion Financial Corp.
