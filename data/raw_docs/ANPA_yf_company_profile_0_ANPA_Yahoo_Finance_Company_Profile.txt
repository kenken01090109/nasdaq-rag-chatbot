Business Summary: Rich Sparkle Holdings Limited operates as a financial printing and corporate services provider in Hong Kong. The company specializes in designing and printing financial print materials. It offers listing documents, financial reports, fund documents, circulars, and announcements. It also provides financial printing services, such as typesetting, proofreading, translation, design, and printing; advisory services, including environmental, social and governance reports; and other services, including provision of co-working space. The company was incorporated in 2016 and is based in Central, Hong Kong.
