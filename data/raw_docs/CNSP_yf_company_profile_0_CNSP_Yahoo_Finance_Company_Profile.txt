Business Summary: CNS Pharmaceuticals, Inc., a clinical pharmaceutical company, develops anti-cancer drug candidates for the treatment of brain and central nervous system (CNS) tumors. The company's drug candidates include TPI 287 and Berubicin, which are in Phase 2 clinical trial for the treatment of glioblastoma and other CNS malignancies. It has license agreement with Cortice Biosciences, Inc. The company was incorporated in 2017 and is headquartered in Houston, Texas.
