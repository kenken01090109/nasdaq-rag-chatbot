Business Summary: Hongli Group Inc., together with its subsidiaries, manufactures and sells customized metal profiles in the People's Republic of China and internationally. The company offers cold roll formed steel profile products in various forms and shapes, such as angles, bows, beams, brackets, channels, cross-members, flanges, hats, panels, plates, posts, rails, stakes, and tracks. It serves mining and excavation, construction, agriculture, and transportation industries. The company was incorporated in 2021 and is headquartered in Weifang, the People's Republic of China. Hongli Group Inc. is a subsidiary of Hongli Development Limited.
