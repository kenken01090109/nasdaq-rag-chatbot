Business Summary: Baiya International Group Inc., through its subsidiaries, provides job matching, entrusted recruitment, project outsourcing, and labor dispatching services to business enterprises and organizations in China. It offers human resource management, payroll and advances, electronic contracts, and pay slips; payment assurance for transactions through its Gongwuyuan Platform; human resource management consulting; labor outsourcing services; staffing and placement services; research and development of apps; logistics and shipping; and storage services. The company also provides education consulting; internet information technology development; and supply chain management services. Baiya International Group Inc. was founded in 2017 and is headquartered in Shenzhen, China.
