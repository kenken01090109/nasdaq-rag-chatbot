Business Summary: Globavend Holdings Limited, together with its subsidiaries, provides integrated cross-border logistics services, and air freight forwarding services in Hong Kong, Australia, and New Zealand. The company's services include parcel consolidation, air freight forwarding, customs clearance, on-carriage parcel transportation, and delivery. It serves e-commerce merchants, as well as operates e-commerce platforms. The company was founded in 2016 and is based in Perth, Australia. Globavend Holdings Limited is a subsidiary of Globavend Investments Limited.
