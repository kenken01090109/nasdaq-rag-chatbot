Business Summary: Enlight Renewable Energy Ltd operates a renewable energy platform in Israel, the Middle East, North Africa, Europe, the United States, and internationally. The company develops, finances, constructs, owns, and operates utility-scale renewable energy projects. It develops wind energy and solar energy projects, as well as energy storage projects. The company holds a portfolio of renewable energy projects that consists of approximately 20 GW of multi-technology generation capacity and 35.8 GWh of energy storage capacity. The company was incorporated in 1981 and is headquartered in Rosh HaAyin, Israel. LT
