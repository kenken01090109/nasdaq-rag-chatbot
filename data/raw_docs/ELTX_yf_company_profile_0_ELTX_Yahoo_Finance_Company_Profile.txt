Business Summary: Elicio Therapeutics, Inc., a clinical-stage biotechnology company, develops immunotherapies for the treatment of cancer. Its lead product candidate is ELI-002, a multivalent lymph nodetargeted amphiphile (AMP) peptide vaccine that is in Phase II clinical trial to target seven Kirsten rat sarcoma viral oncogene homolog driver mutations. The company also develops ELI-007, a multivalent lymph nodetargeted AMP peptide vaccine in preclinical studies for the treatment of mutant b-raf murine sarcoma viral oncogene homolog B1-driven cancers; and ELI-008, a multivalent lymph nodetargeted AMP peptide vaccine in preclinical studies for use in the treatment of mutated tumor protein p53 expressing cancers. Elicio Therapeutics, Inc. is headquartered in Boston, Massachusetts.
