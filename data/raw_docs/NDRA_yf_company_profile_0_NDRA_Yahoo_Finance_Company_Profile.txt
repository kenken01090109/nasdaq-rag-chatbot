Business Summary: ENDRA Life Sciences Inc. develops technologies to improve the capabilities of clinical diagnostic ultrasound. The company is involved in the development of thermo-acoustic enhanced ultrasound technology that uses pulsed energy source comprising radio frequency to transmit energy deep into tissue and generate ultrasonic waves to create high-contrast images for use in the treatment of non-alcoholic fatty liver disease, as well as in tissue composition, temperature monitoring, vascular imaging, and tissue perfusion. ENDRA Life Sciences Inc. was incorporated in 2007 and is based in Ann Arbor, Michigan.
