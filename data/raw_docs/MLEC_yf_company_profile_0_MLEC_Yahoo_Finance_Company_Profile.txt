Business Summary: Moolec Science SA, a science-based ingredient company, engages in the development of ingredients for food, pet food, animal feed, and dietary supplements using molecular farming technology and precision fermentation platforms in Cayman Islands and internationally. It develops nutritional supplements, food ingredients, meat replacements, and by-products using safflowers, chickpeas, fungi, soybeans, and yeast, as well as lupin and peas. The company is based in Grand Cayman, Cayman Islands.
