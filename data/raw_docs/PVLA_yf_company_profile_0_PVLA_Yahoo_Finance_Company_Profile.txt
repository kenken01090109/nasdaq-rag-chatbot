Business Summary: Palvella Therapeutics, Inc., a clinical-stage biopharmaceutical company, focuses on developing and commercializing novel therapies to treat patients serious and rare genetic skin diseases. The company's lead product candidate is QTORIN 3.9% rapamycin anhydrous gel that is in Phase 3 clinical trial for the treatment of microcystic lymphatic malformations, as well as in Phase 2 clinical trial to treat cutaneous venous malformations. It also develops QTORIN rapamycin for the treatment of other mTOR-driven skin diseases. The company is based in Wayne, Pennsylvania
