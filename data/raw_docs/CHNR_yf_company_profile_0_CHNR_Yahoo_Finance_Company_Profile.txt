Business Summary: China Natural Resources, Inc., through its subsidiaries, engages in the exploration and mining of metal properties in the People's Republic of China. It explores for lead, silver, and other nonferrous metals. The company holds interest in the Moruogu Tong mine that covers an area of 7.81 square kilometers located in Wulatehouqi, Bayannaoer City, Inner Mongolia. The company is headquartered in Shenzhen, the People's Republic of China. China Natural Resources, Inc. is a subsidiary of Feishang Industrial Group Co., Ltd.
