Business Summary: YXT.COM Group Holding Limited, through its subsidiaries, provides digital corporate learning solution in the People's Republic of China. The company offers corporate learning platform, personalized e-learning system, teaching tools, and online courses, as well as offline courses and courseware recording service. It also engages in the technology development; and sale of Software-as-a-Service and content. The company was formerly known as Unicentury Group Holding Limited and changed its name to YXT.COM Group Holding Limited in May 2021. The company was founded in 2011 and is headquartered in Suzhou, China.
