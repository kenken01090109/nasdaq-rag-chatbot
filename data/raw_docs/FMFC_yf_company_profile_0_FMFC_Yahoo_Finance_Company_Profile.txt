Business Summary: Kandal M Venture Limited, through its subsidiaries, manufactures, trades in, and sells handbags in the United States, Europe, Canada, Japan, and internationally. It offers shoulder bags, crossbody bags, tote bag, backpacks, top-handle handbags, and satchels; and other smaller leather goods, such as wallets. The company was founded in 2017 and is based in Ta Khmau, the Kingdom of Cambodia. Kandal M Venture Limited operates as a subsidiary of DMD Ventures Limited.
