Business Summary: Good Times Restaurants Inc., through its subsidiaries, engages in the restaurant business in the United States. It owns, operates, and franchises Good Times Burgers & Frozen Custard, a quick-service drive-through dining restaurant; and owns, operates, franchises, and licenses Bad Daddy's Burger Bar, a full-service upscale casual dining restaurant. Good Times Restaurants Inc. was incorporated in 1987 and is based in Golden, Colorado.
