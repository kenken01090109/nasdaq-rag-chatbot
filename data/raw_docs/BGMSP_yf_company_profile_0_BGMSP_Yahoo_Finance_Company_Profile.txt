Business Summary: Bio Green Med Solution, Inc. engages in the distributing, trading, and installation of protective and fire safety materials and equipment, and fire protection and prevention systems. It also researches and develops pharmaceuticals. The company was formerly known as Cyclacel Pharmaceuticals, Inc. and changed its name to Bio Green Med Solution, Inc. in September 2022. The company was founded in 1996 and is headquartered in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.
