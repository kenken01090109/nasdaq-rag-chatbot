Business Summary: Zenta Group Company Limited, together with its subsidiaries, engages in the provision of industrial park consultation and business investment consultation services; and sale of fintech products and services in Macau and the People's Republic of China. It offers fintech services to customers by offering algorithm and big data models and a blockchain system. The company's industrial park consultation services include project development consultation services; agency services of sales and leasing; property management and financial advisory services; advisory on operation improvement services; advisory on selection of suppliers/builders process services; and feasibility study report services. Its business investment consultation services comprise mergers and acquisitions consultation services; and administrative services, such as handling and managing corporate documents, maintaining and updating corporate changes and registrations, providing registered offices, and filing income tax returns. The company was incorporated in 2023 and is based in Macau.
