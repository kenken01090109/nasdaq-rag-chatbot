Business Summary: Autonomix Medical, Inc., a development stage medical device company, focuses on advancing technologies for sensing and treating disorders relating to the peripheral nervous system. The company's technology platform includes a catheter-based microchip-enabled sensing array to detect and differentiate peripheral neural signals. It develops technology for patients with pancreatic cancer and addresses indications for chronic pain management, hypertension, cardiovascular, and other nerve-related disorders. Autonomix Medical, Inc. was incorporated in 2014 and is headquartered in The Woodlands, Texas.
