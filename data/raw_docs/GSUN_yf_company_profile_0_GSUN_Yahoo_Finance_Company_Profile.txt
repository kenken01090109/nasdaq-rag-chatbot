Business Summary: Golden Sun Technology Group Limited, through its subsidiaries, provides education and e-commerce services in China. The company operates through the Education, and E-Commerce and Others segments. It offers foreign language tutorial services and other educational training management services in China. The company also provides logistics, consulting, education-related, marketing, and engineering construction services, as well as focusing on the provision of wellness products. The company was formerly known as Golden Sun Health Technology Group Limited and changed its name to Golden Sun Technology Group Limited in February 2026. Golden Sun Technology Group Limited was founded in 1997 and is based in Shanghai, China.
