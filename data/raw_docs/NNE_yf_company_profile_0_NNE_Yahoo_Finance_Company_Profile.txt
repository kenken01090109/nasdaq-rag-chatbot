Business Summary: NANO Nuclear Energy Inc. operates as a nuclear energy company. The company is developing KRONOS MMR Energy System, a high-temperature gas-cooled reactor; LOKI MMR, a compact microreactor designed for remote and specialized applications; ZEUS, a solid core battery reactor; and ODIN, a low-pressure salt coolant reactor. It is also developing a high-assay low-enriched uranium fuel processing facility to provide a fuel pipeline for the nuclear reactor industry, and fuel transportation and nuclear consultation businesses. The company was incorporated in 2022 and is based in New York, New York.
