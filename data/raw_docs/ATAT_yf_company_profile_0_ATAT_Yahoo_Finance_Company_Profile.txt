Business Summary: Atour Lifestyle Holdings Limited, through its subsidiaries, develops lifestyle brands around hotel offerings in the People's Republic of China. The company provides hotel management services, including day-to-day management services for the hotels on behalf of franchisees, and sells hotel supplies and other products. It also offers retail management services, investment management services, financial information service management, property management services, and software and technology services, as well as operates a travel agency. Atour Lifestyle Holdings Limited was incorporated in 2012 and is headquartered in Shanghai, China.
