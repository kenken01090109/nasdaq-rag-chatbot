Business Summary: Via Renewables, Inc., through its subsidiaries, operates as an independent retail energy services company in the United States. The company engages in the transmission and sale of electricity to residential and commercial customers. It is also involved in the transportation, distribution, and sale of natural gas to residential and commercial customers. In addition, the company provides broker services for retail energy customers; and wireless services and equipment to wireless customers. It offers its services under the Electricity Maine, Electricity N.H., Major Energy, Provider Power Massachusetts, Spark Energy, and Verde Energy brand names. Via Renewables, Inc. was formerly known as Spark Energy, Inc. and changed its name to Via Renewables, Inc. in August 2021. The company was founded in 1999 and is headquartered in Houston, Texas. Via Renewables, Inc operates as a subsidiary of NuRetailco LLC.
