Business Summary: Namib Minerals operates as a gold producer in Africa and Democratic Republic of Congo. The company owns and operates How Mine, Mazowe Mine, and Redwing Mine located in Zimbabwe, Africa. It also holds interest in 13 exploration permits in the Democratic Republic of Congo. The company was incorporated in 2024 and is based in Grand Cayman, Cayman Islands. Namib Minerals operates as a subsidiary of The Southern SelliBen Trust.
