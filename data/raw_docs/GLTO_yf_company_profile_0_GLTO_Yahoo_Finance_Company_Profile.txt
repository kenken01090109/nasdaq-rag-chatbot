Business Summary: Galecto, Inc., a clinical-stage biotechnology company, develops molecule therapeutics for the treatment of fibrosis, cancer, inflammation, and other related diseases. The company develops GB3226, a preclinical small molecule dual inhibitor of ENL-YEATS and FLT3 and GB1211, an oral small molecule inhibitor of galectin-3 to treat multiple types of oncology and liver disease indications. Galecto, Inc. was founded in 2011 and is headquartered in Copenhagen, Denmark.
