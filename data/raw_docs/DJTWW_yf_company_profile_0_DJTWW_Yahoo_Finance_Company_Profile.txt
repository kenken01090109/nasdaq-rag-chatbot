Business Summary: Trump Media & Technology Group Corp. engages in social media and streaming services in the United States. It operates Truth Social, a social media platform for free expression; and Truth+, a streaming platform focusing on family-friendly live TV channels and on-demand content. The company also offers Truth.Fi, a financial service and FinTech brand that incorporates America First investment vehicles; and a digital asset strategy including a bitcoin treasury. Trump Media & Technology Group Corp. was founded in 2021 and is headquartered in Sarasota, Florida.
