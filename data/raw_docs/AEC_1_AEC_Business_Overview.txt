Company: Anfield Energy Inc. Common Shares
Ticker: AEC
Business Summary: Anfield Energy Inc. engages in the exploration, evaluation, development, and production of mineral properties in the United States. It primarily explores for vanadium, uranium, and gold deposits. The company was formerly known as Anfield Resources Inc. and changed its name to Anfield Energy Inc. in December 2017. Anfield Energy Inc. was incorporated in 1989 and is headquartered in Burnaby, Canada.
