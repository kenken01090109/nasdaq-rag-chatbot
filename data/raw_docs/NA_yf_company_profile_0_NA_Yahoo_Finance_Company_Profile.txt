Business Summary: Nano Labs Ltd operates as a fabless integrated circuit design company and product solution provider in the People's Republic of China, Hong Kong, Singapore, the United States and internationally. The company develops computing chips. It also provides application specific integrated circuit chips, smart network interface cards, and vision computing chips. In addition, the company engages in the research and development of software. Its customers include enterprises and individual buyers. Nano Labs Ltd was founded in 2019 and is headquartered in Hangzhou, the People's Republic of China.
