Long Business Summary: Allied Gaming & Entertainment Inc., through its subsidiaries, operates as a public esports and entertainment company worldwide. The company also engages in the development and distribution of mobile casual games; organization of events, shows, and concerts; and operation of a flagship gaming arena located at the Luxor Hotel in Las Vegas, Nevada. In addition, it offers management and consultation services to experiential entertainment venue operation. The company is based in New York, New York.
