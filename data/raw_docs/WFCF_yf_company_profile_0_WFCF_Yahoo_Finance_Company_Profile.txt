Business Summary: Where Food Comes From, Inc., together with its subsidiaries, operates as a third-party food verification company in North America. The company conducts on-site and desk audits to verify claims about livestock, food, other specialty crops, and agricultural and aquaculture products; offers various value-added services, including verification, certification, consulting, and other professional services; and sells hardware and technology solutions. It serves farmers, ranchers, vineyards, wineries, processors, retailers, distributors, trade associations, consumer brands, chefs, and restaurants, as well as beef and pork packers, organic producers and processors, and specialty retail chains. The company was formerly known as Integrated Management Information, Inc. and changed its name to Where Food Comes From, Inc. in December 2012. Where Food Comes From, Inc. was founded in 1996 and is based in Castle Rock, Colorado.
