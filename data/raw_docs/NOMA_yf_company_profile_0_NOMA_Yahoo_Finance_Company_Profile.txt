Business Summary: Nomadar Corp., a sport technology business, focuses on developing and operating sport technology platforms. It offers high performance training programs for young athletes in soccer programs. The company also operates the Mirandilla Stadium and holds trademark rights to the Mágico González brand. It serves professional sports teams, athletes, coaches, recreational sports enthusiasts, and end user customers. The company was formerly known as Sportech City USA, Corp. and changed its name to Nomadar Corp. in December 2023. The company was incorporated in 2023 and is based in Marshall, Texas. Nomadar Corp. operates as a subsidiary of Sport City Cádiz S.L.
