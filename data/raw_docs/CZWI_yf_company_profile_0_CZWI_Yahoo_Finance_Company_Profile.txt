Business Summary: Citizens Community Bancorp, Inc. operates as a bank holding company for Citizens Community Federal N.A. that provides various traditional community banking services to businesses, agricultural operators, and consumers in the United States. The company accepts various deposit products, including demand deposits, savings and money market accounts, and certificates of deposit. It also provides various loan products, such as commercial real estate, commercial and industrial, agricultural real estate, agricultural operating, and consumer loans; and one-to-four family residential mortgages and home equity lines-of-credit. In addition, the company maintains a portfolio of investments comprising mortgage-backed, corporate asset-backed, U.S. Government sponsored agency, and corporate debt securities. The company operates in Wisconsin, Minnesota and Other locations. Citizens Community Bancorp, Inc. was founded in 1938 and is based in Eau Claire, Wisconsin.
