Business Summary: EverQuote, Inc. operates an online marketplace for insurance shopping in the United States. The company provides automotive, and home and renters insurance, as well as campaign management tools. The company serves insurance carriers and agents, and indirect distributors. The company was formerly known as AdHarmonics, Inc., and changed its name to EverQuote, Inc. in November 2014. EverQuote, Inc. was incorporated in 2008 and is based in Cambridge, Massachusetts.
