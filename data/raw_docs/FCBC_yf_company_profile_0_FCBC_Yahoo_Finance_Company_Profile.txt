Business Summary: First Community Bankshares, Inc. operates as the financial holding company for First Community Bank that provides various banking products and services. It offers demand deposit accounts, savings and money market accounts, certificates of deposit, and individual retirement plans; and commercial, consumer real estate, and consumer and other loans. The company also provides trust management, estate administration, and investment advisory services. It serves individuals and businesses across various industries, such as education, government, and health services; retail trade; construction; manufacturing; tourism; coal mining and gas extraction; and transportation. The company operates through branches in West Virginia, Virginia, North Carolina, and Tennessee. First Community Bankshares, Inc. was founded in 1874 and is headquartered in Bluefield, Virginia.
