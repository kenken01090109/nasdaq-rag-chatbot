Business Summary: Republic Power Group Limited, through its subsidiary, Republic Power Pte Ltd., provides customized enterprise resource planning (ERP) software solutions, consulting and technical support services, and peripheral hardware to corporate clients and government agencies in Singapore and Malaysia. The company's ERP solutions include accounting, procurement, workflow automation capabilities, monitoring, and resources allocation, as well as planning surveillance and threat detection. It serves trading, logistics, and property management industries. The company was incorporated in 2015 and is headquartered in Singapore. Republic Power Group Limited operates as a subsidiary of True Sage International Limited.
