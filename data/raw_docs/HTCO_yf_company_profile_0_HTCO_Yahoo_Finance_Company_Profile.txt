Business Summary: High-Trend International Group provides ocean transportation services in Singapore, Dubai, South Korea, Japan, India, and rest of Asia. The company offers shipping and logistics support services. It also provides seaborne transportation services under voyage contracts; vessel services on behalf of ship owners; and heating services. The company was formerly known as Caravelle International Group and changed its name to High-Trend International Group in January 2025. High-Trend International Group was founded in 2021 and is headquartered in Singapore.
